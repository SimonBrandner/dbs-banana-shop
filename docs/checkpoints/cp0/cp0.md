## The Banana Shop where the Bananas Are Free

A **customer** may come into the shop and pick **bananas** they want to get. They then may place an **order** containing those **bananas**. An **order** is processed by an **employee**. Each **banana** is associated with a **shelf** on which it is displayed. The **shelf** determines the price per kilogram of the bananas in it, which may or may not be zero (not all the bananas are free). Each **banana** has a weight. However, some **customers** have some of the bananas for free (all the bananas are not free to everybody).

## Motivation

James Acaster, a British comedian, once went to a shop and wanted to purchase a single banana. Once he was ready to pay for his banana, he met his great nemesis - _The Cashier Lady._ Apparently the shop was giving away free bananas. James then said: "Thanks very much", just for him to learn that the situation might not be so simple.

It's time for the revenge. And that is our destiny.
